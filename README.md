# Travel Anxiety Management Framework
Systematic approach to managing travel anxiety and building travel confidence — from trigger identification to graduated exposure.
## What it does
- Identifies **anxiety triggers** through structured worksheets covering flight anxiety, social anxiety, and unfamiliar environments
- Builds a **coping strategy toolkit** with breathing exercises, grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method), and in-trip distraction libraries
- Designs **confidence building exercises** including graduated exposure plans, pre-trip cognitive restructuring, and post-trip reflection journals
## Example scenarios
**1. 第一次出国自由行很紧张**
> 👤 第一次自己去日本玩7天，已经订好机票酒店了但还是很担心，语言不通怎么办？
> 🤖 输出出国焦虑管理清单——行前准备（离线翻译+离线地图+国际漫游/SIM卡）、落地后（机场拿中文地图、大站有中文指示）、日常（扫码点餐、支付宝/微信支付）、应急（使领馆电话+急救119），核心建议：焦虑来自未知，提前模拟每一步能大大降低不安。推荐用苹果查找App分享位置给家人。

**2. 14-hour flight anxiety**
> 👤 I have flight anxiety and a 14-hour trip coming up. Build me a coping plan.
> 🤖 Pre-flight preparation checklist (seat selection, airport familiarity, medication consultation), in-flight coping toolkit (breathing exercises, distraction library, trigger-point acupressure), and post-flight decompression plan — with anxiety-level tracking template.

**3. Travel anxiety template for therapists**
> 👤 Create a general travel anxiety management template for my therapy clients.
> 🤖 Therapist-friendly framework — anxiety trigger identification worksheet, pre-trip cognitive restructuring exercises, in-trip grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method), safety-behavior reduction plan, and post-trip reflection journal prompts.
